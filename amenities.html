Five Star Trail - Amenities Overview



Rest rooms water: In Lynch Field, Chemical toilets at some trailhead in season.

Bike shop, rental: Several bike shops in Greensburg.

Restaurant, groceries: Several restaurants within a block of the trail in Greensburg. Many restaurants along US119. Lots of chain fast-food places near Huff Av. Convenience stores within a few blocks of Lynch Field and the Railroad Museum.

Camping, simple lodging: Motels in the Greensburg and Youngwood, including one adjacent to the trail near Huff Av. Camping about 1.4 miles northwest of Youngwood

Swimming, fishing: Swimming pool at Lynch Field.

Wheelchair access: All trailheads accessible, but thereâ€™s a steep grade from Lynch Field to the railroad underpass. There are several street crossings with moderate traffic.
